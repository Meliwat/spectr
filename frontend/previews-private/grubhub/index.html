Dark Mode Notes

Grubhub's dark mode keeps a warm near-black canvas at #121212; cards lift to #1C1C1E. Grubhub Red #F63440 stays the primary action color across themes — it stays vivid on dark. Deal text shifts to Orange #FF8000 and the live-tracking accent is Track Blue #00A0DF. Food photography is never dimmed — only chrome, dividers and surfaces darken so the dishes stay appetizing.
