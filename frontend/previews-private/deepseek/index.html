If a train leaves at 3pm going 60mph and another at 4pm going 80mph, when do they meet?
DeepSeek-R1
Thought for 14 seconds · tap to collapse
Let me set up the problem. Train A starts at 3pm at 60mph. By 4pm it has a 60-mile head start. Train B starts at 4pm at 80mph, closing the gap at 80 − 60 = 20mph. Time to close 60 miles: 60 ÷ 20 = 3 hours. So they meet at 4pm + 3h = 7pm…
They meet at 7:00 PM. Train B needs 3 hours after 4pm to close Train A's 60-mile head start (20 mph closing speed).

Dark Mode Notes

DeepSeek's dark mode is a near-black #0E0E10 canvas with a single electric accent — DeepSeek Blue #4D6BFE — used sparingly on the whale mark, the user bubble (soft #1E2240 fill), the send button, and the active DeepThink / Search pills. The signature element is the reasoning trace: a recessed #16171A panel (darker than the canvas) with a 2pt blue left-bar, holding the R1 model's chain-of-thought in dimmed italic #8A8B90. The trace is collapsible and visually subordinate to the final answer, which sits at full-contrast #ECECEC.
