Dark Mode Notes

Vrbo's dark mode is a cool blue-charcoal: canvas at #101317, cards lifting to #181C22. Vrbo Blue #245ABC brightens to #4F8BF0 for links, pins, and outline buttons so it reads on dark; the primary "Book now" fill stays the deeper brand blue. The single gold review star #F2B01E is the only warm pixel and is held constant across themes. Listing photography remains full-color and full-bleed — only the chrome dims.
