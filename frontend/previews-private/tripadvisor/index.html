Light Mode Notes

Tripadvisor's canvas is pure #FFFFFF — a clean gallery wall, never tinted. Grouped cards lift to #F2F2F2. Tripadvisor Green #34E0A1 is the only accent: primary action, active tab, and the five-circle bubble rating. Black text sits on the green CTA intentionally — the mint is bright enough that black maximizes contrast. Traveler photography supplies all the warmth; UI chrome stays out of the way.
