Dark Mode Notes

Canva's product is famously near-white; its dark mode keeps the canvas warm-neutral at #18191B rather than pure black, with panels lifting to #1F2023. The cyan→purple brand gradient (#00C4CC → #7D2AE8) is identical across themes — it is the create FAB, the primary button and the logo, and it pops on dark. Pro features stay gold #FFC24B. Template thumbnails and user designs remain full-color — only the surrounding chrome dims, never the creative content.
