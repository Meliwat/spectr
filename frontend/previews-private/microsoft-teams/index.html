Dual Identity Notes

Teams is genuinely dual-identity — light (#F5F5F5 canvas / #FFFFFF cards) and dark (#1F1F1F / #2D2C2C) are both first-class. Teams Purple shifts from #6264A7 (light) to a lighter #5B5FC7 (dark) for contrast. Presence dots — green available, red busy/DND, yellow away, gray offline — are the only place Teams allows loaded semantic color; people scan them constantly. Fluent elevation is soft and low-contrast in light, replaced by surface steps in dark. The meeting join bar is the priority CTA when a meeting is live and pulses subtly.
