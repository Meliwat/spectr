Dark Mode Notes

Deliveroo's dark mode keeps a warm near-black canvas at #121212; cards lift to #1C1C1E. Deliveroo Teal #00CCBC is the single brand color and stays identical across themes — it glows on dark, so its on-color flips to deep teal-ink #003733 for legible button labels. The kangaroo mark, ratings, Plus and active tab all share this one teal. Food photography is never dimmed — only chrome and dividers darken.
