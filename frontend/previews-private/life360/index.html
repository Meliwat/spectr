Dark Mode Notes

Life360's dark mode is a deep violet night, canvas #161325 with a dark-styled map (#1A2138 base, muted roads and parks) so the colored member pins pop. Life360 Purple #7E57C2 stays the brand spine across themes — Circle selector, Places geofences, primary CTAs. The defining rule is member identity color: every family member is assigned a fixed hue (amber, teal, pink, blue, purple) that is the same on their map pin, their bottom-sheet avatar, and their detail screen — color is how you find your kid on the map. SOS red #FF6B6B and Safe green #34C759 are reserved for safety state only.
