Dark Mode Notes

Vero is true-black-first — the canvas is pure #000000, giving photography an OLED-deep, gallery-wall presentation. Surfaces lift only barely to #0E0E10 / #161618 so cards are felt, not framed. The brand is a single teal→blue gradient (#00D1C1 → #0079D3) used on the wordmark, the primary Post CTA, the compose ring, and active states; the flat #00C2B8 teal carries small accents like liked hearts and active post-type icons. The chronological, no-algorithm feed and the 7-type post selector (photo / video / link / music / film / book / place) are the soul of the product — calm, deliberate, anti-doomscroll.
