happn is built around one idea: the people you've crossed paths with in real life. The accent is happn pink #FF4865 fading toward magenta #E91E63; type is the rounded-geometric Poppins over a near-black graphite canvas.

Dark Mode Notes

happn's dark theme uses a cool graphite canvas #0E0E12 (not pure black) so the connector timeline and rounded cards read with subtle depth. happn pink #FF4865 stays identical across themes — it carries the Charm heart, the timeline dots, the active tab, and the primary CTA. The pink→magenta gradient (#FF4865 → #E91E63) appears on hero buttons and the Crush star. Gold #FFC24B is reserved exclusively for Crush/premium. Cards lift from #18181F; dividers and the timeline spine sit at #2A2A33.
