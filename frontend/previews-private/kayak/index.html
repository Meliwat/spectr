Dark Mode Notes

KAYAK's dark canvas is near-neutral (#101214) with no brand tint — KAYAK Orange #FF690F is the single accent and must stay loud against it (selected dates, hacker-fare tags, the primary "View Deal" CTA, the active tab). The price-signal ramp is functional: green #1E9E5A for low, neutral text for mid, red #E5484D for high — used in the calendar strip and matrix. Forecast advice uses buy/wait/rise semantics (green/amber/red). Cards lift to #181B1E; shadows deepen to 0.4–0.5 opacity. Airline logos keep their brand color; chrome dims.
