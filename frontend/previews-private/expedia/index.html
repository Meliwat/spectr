Dark Mode Notes

Expedia's dark canvas carries a subtle navy bias (#0E1116) that echoes the brand's deep #00355F blue; cards lift to #161B22. Expedia Yellow #FFC94D stays identical across themes — it is the deal/savings signal and reads vividly on dark. Action Blue #1668E3 lightens its perceived contrast on dark surfaces but the hex holds. One Key gold #F5C518 is reserved exclusively for rewards balances and earn lines. Property photography stays full-color; only chrome dims.
