Dark Mode Notes

Amazon Music is dark-first. The canvas is a deep teal-navy #0C1B22 — not neutral grey — which gives the app its distinct, oceanic identity versus Spotify or YT Music. The full-screen player sits on a top-down gradient that lifts toward #16404C behind the art. Amazon Cyan #00A8E1 is the structural accent for play/scrubber; the brighter #25D1DA is used for the active X-Ray lyric line and the active tab. Album art and the X-Ray panel are the visual center; chrome stays low-contrast.
