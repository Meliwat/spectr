Dark Mode Notes

Bandcamp is fundamentally a light, editorial product — paper-white #FFFFFF / #F4F4F4 with big square album art as the hero, because the artwork (uploaded by the artist) is the brand, not the chrome. Bandcamp Teal #1DA0C3 is scarce: it marks artist links, the buy/support price, and collection accents — the "support the artist" path. In dark mode the canvas inverts to a cool charcoal #101417 with surfaces at #1A2024; teal shifts slightly toward #3DB5D6 for contrast, text inverts to #EDF1F3, and album art stays full-color and untinted — content never dims, only chrome does.
