Calm-By-Design Notes

Things deliberately uses more whitespace than competitors — roomy 44pt rows, 20pt margins, big calm titles. The white room #FFFFFF is flat: no card shadows, no skeuomorphic depth. The only intentional shadow is the soft blue glow under the Magic-Plus. Things Blue #4F97FF is the single action color; the one warm note is the #FFD60A Today star. The circular-checkbox fill (center-out, then a spring checkmark, then a gentle fade-out) is the signature delight — completion is never abrupt. A true dark mode exists at #1C1C1E, but the white room is the identity.
