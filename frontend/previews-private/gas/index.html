Light Mode Notes

Gas is intentionally a light, joyful app — there is no dark mode in spirit. The screen is a full-bleed indigo→purple gradient (#6C5CE7 → #A06CFF) with a big, friendly white poll card (#FFFFFF, 28px radius, soft purple-tinted shadow) floating at the center. The four name choices are bold gradient pills in a fixed rotating palette (indigo, pink, yellow, green) so every poll feels bright and game-like. The flame 🔥 (#FF7A45) is the app's currency and dopamine loop — earned by voting, spent to unlock "who picked you" hints. Type is Nunito at heavy weights (800–900) for a rounded, middle-school-friendly, anti-corporate feel. Everything is positive-only, anonymous, and playful.
