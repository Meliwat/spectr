Dark Mode Notes

Plex is dark-first and intentionally not true black — the canvas is a cool charcoal #1F2326 so poster art reads as warm against a neutral, calm ground. Surfaces lift to #282C30 / #32373B for the server picker and rows. Plex Yellow #E5A00D is the single accent and always pairs with dark ink #1A1304 on filled buttons (never white-on-yellow). The yellow appears only on the Play button, the active tab, the On Deck progress bar, the unwatched corner dot, and the selected-server check. Server-status dots use green #4CAF50 (online) / gray #6B7075 (offline).
