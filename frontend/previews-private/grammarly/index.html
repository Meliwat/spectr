Dark Mode Notes

Grammarly's dark mode keeps a warm charcoal canvas #121212 with cards lifted to #1C1C1E — never pure black. Grammarly Green #15C39A is the single brand accent and is identical across themes; on dark it stays the CTA fill, the score ring, and the assistant orb. The four suggestion-underline hues (red #E5484D, blue #3B82F6, green #16A34A, purple #8B5CF6) are slightly brightened on dark so they remain legible under body text. The document text inverts to #E4E4E4 to preserve the calm, editorial reading surface — the editor is the product, so its chrome dims while the prose does not.
