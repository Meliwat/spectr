Character.AI iOS — Design Tokens
Persona Dark

Character.AI is a near-black canvas (#0F0F10) built for one thing: chatting with a persona. The signature screen is the conversation — a circular character avatar, a centered greeting, asymmetric AI/user bubbles with a tucked top corner, italic *roleplay actions*, and a typing indicator. The single accent is a calm blue #3A7BFD; type is the geometric Sora over Inter for message legibility.

Dark Mode Notes

Character.AI is dark-first — the canvas is a near-black neutral #0F0F10 so the conversation, not chrome, holds attention. AI and user bubbles are deliberately low-contrast greys (#1C1C1F vs #26262A) differentiated by the asymmetric tucked corner (4px on the speaker side, 18px elsewhere) rather than by color — the accent blue #3A7BFD is reserved for the send button, primary CTAs, and links, never the bubbles. Italic *roleplay actions* render in muted secondary text. The character avatar (radial blue or lilac gradient) is the one warm focal point on every screen.
