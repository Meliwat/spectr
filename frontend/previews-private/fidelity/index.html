Dark Mode Notes

Fidelity's dark mode is a deep evergreen, not pure black — canvas sits at #0E1411 with cards lifting to #16201B and #1F2C24. Fidelity Green #368727 stays the action color; the brighter #4FB23B Leaf handles selected tabs and links where contrast on the dark field matters. Semantic finance color is sacred: gains are always #15B374, losses always #E5544B, and they never swap or fade. Numerals use a tabular monospaced face so dollar columns and percentages align perfectly. Charts keep a green stroke with a low-opacity green fill — the only place a gradient appears.
