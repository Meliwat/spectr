Dark Mode Notes

Goodreads' light identity is warm tan paper #F4F1EA with brown ink #382110. Dark mode inverts the paper to a warm ink-brown canvas #161310 (never pure black — it would kill the cozy library feel); cards lift to #211C16. The rating Amber #E9A100 is held identical across themes — it is the brand's load-bearing signal on every cover and review. Brown becomes a warm tan-gold accent #C9883D for active tabs against the dark canvas, and link teal lightens to #6FB3AD for contrast. Book covers always render full-color; only chrome dims.
