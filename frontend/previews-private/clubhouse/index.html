The whole product is audio — the UI is a stage of faces, not a feed.

Dark Mode Notes

Clubhouse's light identity is the warm cream "hallway" (#F2EFE4) with ink text (#1A1A1A). In dark, the cream inverts to a warm near-black room (#1A1A1A) — never a cold gray — and cards lift to #242220. The Speaking Emerald (#38B569) stays identical across themes: it is the single source of "who is talking", rendered as a 3px ring plus a soft rgba(56,181,105,0.18) pulse halo. Avatar gradients keep their full warmth; muted speakers drop to 55% opacity. Every control is a pill — there are almost no rectangles in Clubhouse.
