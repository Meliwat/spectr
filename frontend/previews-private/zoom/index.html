In-Call Theater Notes

Outside a call Zoom follows light/dark appearance; inside a call it snaps to the committed dark theater #1A1A1A so participant video is the brightest element. Zoom Blue #2D8CFF is the only accent — the oversized Join button, active controls, links. In-call red #E02828 means exactly two things: Leave/End and a muted microphone. The active speaker is lifted by a 3pt blue inset border (debounced ~300ms so it never flickers), and the floating control bar auto-hides after ~4s of no interaction.
