Dark Mode Notes

Vimeo is dark-first — the app is a screening room. Canvas sits at near-black #0D0E12 so the 16:9 player and thumbnails read as the light source; cards lift only one step to #16181F. Vimeo Blue #00ADEF is the sole interactive accent (the legacy #1AB7EA still appears in older surfaces) and is reserved for the scrubber fill, primary CTAs, and active tabs. Staff Gold #FFD24C exclusively marks curated picks. Video frames are never dimmed — only chrome recedes.
