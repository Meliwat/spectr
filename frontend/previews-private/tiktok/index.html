Why No Light Mode?

TikTok's entire design thesis is the full-bleed 9:16 video feed. The app's chrome exists to disappear against bright or dark video frames with equal grace — a white canvas would blow out every bright video frame and defeat the point. Every screen (feed, profile, inbox, discover, settings) ships on the true-black canvas. The only "light" moments in the product are the camera viewfinder (which shows the user's own image) and the effect picker (which is modal and doesn't persist).

This file is intentionally the same dark catalog as preview.html — there is no light counterpart to render.
