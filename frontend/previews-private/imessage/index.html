Dark Mode Notes

iMessage uses a true-black canvas (#000000) in dark mode for OLED efficiency. The signature outgoing iMessage Blue #007AFF and SMS Green #34C759 are identical across themes — they're brand-defining and read well on both. Incoming bubbles invert from light gray #E9E9EB to dark gray #26262A. Text inverts to pure white #FFFFFF, with metadata at #98989F. The blur-backed input bar dims; bubble tints and content do not.
