Dark Mode Notes

Resy is dark-first by design — the canvas is pure black #000000 to make full-color restaurant photography and the editorial serif headlines pop like a printed magazine. Surfaces lift to #121212 / #1C1C1C. Resy Red #C73E3A stays the single reservation color; it brightens to #E2504B for active tabs and emphasis on black. Available slots are red-outlined, the recommended slot is solid red, and Notify uses amber #D99A2B with a dashed border. A light mode inverts to white, but black is the brand.
