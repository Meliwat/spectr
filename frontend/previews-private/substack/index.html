Dark Mode Notes

Substack's reading surface is paper-white #FFFFFF with near-black serif ink #1F1F1F by default. Dark mode (Reader theme) inverts the page to a soft charcoal canvas #121212 — never pure black — with body ink at #EDEDED; cards lift to #1B1B1B. Substack Orange #FF6719 is held identical across themes — it is the single load-bearing signal for the Subscribe CTA, active tab, and unread dot. Publication header art and avatars stay full-color; only chrome and the page dim. The orange is used sparingly: one Subscribe button per post, never as decoration.
