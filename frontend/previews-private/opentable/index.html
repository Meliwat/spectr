Dark Mode Notes

OpenTable's dark mode keeps a clean neutral canvas #121212 with cards at #1C1C1E. OpenTable Red #DA3743 is slightly muted on dark, so the bright variant #F2545B carries the Reserve CTA, active tab, and selected time slot. The teal Points accent #1F8A8A and gold review star #E8A33D stay vivid for trust and reward signals. Restaurant photography stays full-color — the booking flow chrome dims, the food does not.
