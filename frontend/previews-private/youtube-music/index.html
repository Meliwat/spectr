Dark Mode Notes

YouTube Music is dark-only on iOS. Canvas sits at near-black #030303 — deliberately darker than Spotify's #121212 so the immersive Now Playing backdrop (a blurred, scaled copy of the album art) reads as a glow. The Song/Video toggle pill is the signature control. Primary playback is a #FFFFFF circle — YT Music Red #FF0000 is reserved for brand chrome, subscribe CTAs and the active scrubber, never the play button itself. Album art is never dimmed; only chrome recedes.
