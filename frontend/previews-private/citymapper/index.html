Dark Mode Notes

Citymapper's dark canvas is a deep blue-black #0C0E14 so the map and electric blue #2B5BFF pop. The transit-mode palette is the soul of the app and is theme-invariant: walk #00B894, bus #E8453C, tube #2B5BFF, rail #8E44D8, bike #00A8C5, cab #FFB400 stay identical light and dark — a bus is always red. The GO button is a fat #00C281 green pill, the single most recognizable control, unchanged across every screen and theme. Cards lift via #15171F → #1E212B rather than shadows.
