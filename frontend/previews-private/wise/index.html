Light Mode Notes

Wise is bright and open with a single dark anchor — the forest account hero at #0E2200. Forest #163300 is the structural ink for titles and primary numbers; Bright Green #9FE870 is action and highlight, always carrying forest text, never white — that contrast is the brand. The itemized fee-transparency card with the highlighted "Recipient gets" line in #EAF9DC is the signature object: the cost is never hidden. Every figure is tabular and decimal-aligned.
