Plan + Pace

Runna pairs a deep training Indigo (#4F46E5) with an electric Lime (#C2F94E) for energy and "go" actions. The two signatures are the training-plan calendar — a week strip of color-coded run days — and the guided run-session card with its structured workout breakdown (warm-up → intervals → cooldown) and pace targets.

Dark Mode Notes

Runna's app is dark-first — athletes train at dawn and dusk, and a dark UI keeps the screen low-glare on a wrist or armband. Canvas is a deep blue-black #0E0E16 (tinted toward Indigo, never neutral); cards lift to #181826. The two brand colors carry every accent: Indigo #4F46E5 for plan structure and primary surfaces, electric Lime #C2F94E for "go" — the Start button and the today marker. Run-type colors (Easy #34D399, Tempo #FBBF24, Intervals #FB7185, Long #4F46E5) are fixed across themes so athletes read the week at a glance. Lime always pairs with near-black text for contrast.
