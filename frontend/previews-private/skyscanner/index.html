Dark Mode Notes

Skyscanner's dark canvas is a cool navy-black #0B0F14, not pure black — it keeps the brand's aviation feel. Sky Blue #0770E3 stays the primary action color across themes; on dark it gains a brighter sibling #05A8FA for links and the Everywhere accent. The traffic-light price system is sacred: green #00A698, amber #FFB81C, red #E5392E never shift hue between light and dark, because a "good deal" must always look the same. Cards lift from canvas via #141A22 → #1C242E rather than shadows.
