Dark Mode Notes

Miro's dark mode keeps the board a deep neutral #202024 with a subtle dotted grid in #38383F — sticky notes stay full-color pastels (paper doesn't go dark) so the work pops. Miro Yellow #FFD02F and Miro Blue selection #4262FF are identical across themes. Chrome (toolbar, top bar, sheets) recedes to #26262B; text inverts to #ECECEF. Multiplayer cursors keep their per-user colors on both themes.
