Dark Mode Notes

POF's dark canvas sits at #121417 — a cool near-black, never pure black — with cards lifting to #1A1D21. POF Blue (#0098DB → #00A6E2) is identical across themes: it's the logo, the like button, the active tab and the primary CTA, and it pops over dark. The online indicator stays teal #00C9B7; unread message badges stay pink #FF4F8B; upgrade prompts stay gold #FFB23E. Profile photos remain full-color over the dark match grid — only the surrounding chrome dims.
