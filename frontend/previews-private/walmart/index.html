Light Mode Notes

Walmart is a bright, high-contrast store optimized for in-aisle and outdoor readability. Canvas stays pure white #FFFFFF; grouped modules sit on a cool tint #F2F8FD. Walmart Blue #0071DC is the single action color — buttons, links, the active tab. Spark Yellow #FFC220 is ritual: the six-ray logo and the Rollback savings tag, always with dark #2E2F32 text, never white. Separation is border-first (1px #E2E8F0), shadow-second and faint.
