Dark Mode Notes

Revolut is a cool near-black cockpit, never warm and never pure #000000. Canvas sits at #0A0A0F; surfaces lift to #16161F / #1E1E2A as floating slabs. The violet→purple gradient #5B6BFF → #9C6BFF is the entire brand accent — CTA, active tab, the spend arc, the card sheen — and the primary CTA glows with a soft rgba(108,91,255,0.30) halo. Solid #6B5BFF appears only where a gradient can't render. Every money figure is tabular and decimal-aligned.
