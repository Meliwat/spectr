Dark Mode Notes

GroupMe's dark mode keeps the brand energetic. The chat nav bar stays full GroupMe Blue #00AFF0 — it reads great on both themes and anchors group identity. Canvas sits at #121212; inbound bubbles lift to #2A2A2C while outbound bubbles stay brand blue. The heart "like" pill docks below a bubble in #1C1C1E with the count and a #FF3B5C heart. Per-group themes recolor avatars and accents, but the blue chrome and like interaction remain consistent.
