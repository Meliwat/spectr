Pure Monochrome

Raya is uncompromisingly monochrome: pure black (#000000) and pure white (#FFFFFF), with no brand hue at all. The signature is a full-bleed slideshow profile set to the member's chosen song — segmented story bars, a music ticker, and an editorial caption. Color comes only from the member's photography; every UI element is black, white, or a gray between.

Dark Mode Notes

Raya has no light mode and no brand color — it is permanently, deliberately monochrome. The canvas is pure black #000000 (not a warm near-black); the only "accent" is pure white #FFFFFF, used for the wordmark, the single primary button, the active story bar, and the heart outline. Surfaces lift only to #0D0D0D/#161616; structure is drawn with hairlines (#2E2E2E), never shadows. The only color in the entire experience is the member's own full-bleed photography and video, which the UI deliberately recedes behind via a bottom black scrim. The signature slideshow plays the member's chosen song with a small white music ticker and segmented story bars.
