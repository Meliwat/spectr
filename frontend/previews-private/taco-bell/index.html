Dark Mode Notes

Taco Bell's app is dark-first — the canvas is a purple-tinted near-black (#0E0A14), not neutral gray, so the brand purple feels native rather than applied. The signature #702082 → #C72BC8 purple-to-magenta gradient drives every primary CTA, active step, and accent. Hot Sauce Yellow #FFC700 is the high-energy highlight for prices, rewards, and eyebrows. Surfaces lift on a purple bias (#1A1322, #261B32) and food imagery stays vivid against the neon chrome.
