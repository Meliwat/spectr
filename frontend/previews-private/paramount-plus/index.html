Dark Mode Notes

Paramount+ is dark-only on a deep navy canvas #0A0E2D — not pure black — that subtly lightens to #0E1438 under hero art, echoing the brand's blue mountain. Paramount+ Blue #0064FF is the single accent: it fills every primary CTA ("Play"), tints the active tab, and powers progress bars. The brand-hubs row (CBS, MTV, Nickelodeon, Comedy Central, Showtime) sits high on Home, signalling that this is a bundle of networks, each hub a network-brand-colored chip. Live TV and sports — a core Paramount+ pillar — are flagged with red #FF2D46 and a pulsing dot. Poster and key art stay full-color; navy chrome recedes so the catalog leads.
