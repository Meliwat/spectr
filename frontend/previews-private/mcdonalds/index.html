Dark Mode Notes

McDonald's dark mode keeps a near-black canvas (#121212) with cards lifting to #1C1C1E. Golden Arches Yellow #FFC72C is unchanged across themes and always pairs with near-black text (#1A1A1A) for the contrast the brand requires — never white text on yellow. McDonald's Red #DA291C stays vivid for deal flags and the secondary CTA. The rewards points number glows in yellow; food photography remains full-color while the dark chrome recedes.
