Dark Mode Notes

Airbnb's dark mode is warm, not pure black. Canvas sits at #121212; cards lift to #1C1C1E. Primary Coral #FF385C is identical across themes — the pink-red reads beautifully on both light and dark. Text inverts from Hof #484848 to #DDDDDD, preserving the warm, editorial feel. Photography remains full-color — UI chrome dims, content does not.
