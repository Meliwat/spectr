Light Mode Notes

eBay's canvas is pure #FFFFFF — a dense catalog tuned for browse velocity, not editorial warmth. The four brand colors are used with discipline: eBay Blue #0064D2 = action and links, eBay Red #E53238 = urgency and the watch-heart, yellow/green are accents. The bid-vs-Buy-It-Now badge is the signature — the visual contrast between a blue "Buy It Now" tag and a neutral "12 bids" tag is the buy-mechanic information. "Time left" turns red under 24h with tabular seconds that never jitter. Badges are 4pt-radius tags, squarer than the full-pill CTAs. Separation is whitespace + hairlines, not heavy shadow.
