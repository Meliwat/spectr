Dark Mode Notes

Noom is light-first and cheerful, but its dark mode keeps the brand energy. Canvas drops to #121212 with cards at #1C1C1E. Noom Blue #2A5DF6 stays the primary action color and reads well on dark. The food-color system is sacrosanct — Green #34C759, Yellow #FFC531, Red #FF5A52 never change hue across themes because users learn to read food at a glance by color. The lesson card keeps its blue→teal gradient; only the surrounding chrome dims.
