Dark Mode Notes

Monzo's dark mode is deep navy, not pure black — canvas sits at #0E1620, derived from the brand navy #14233C which becomes the card-and-row surface. Hot Coral #FF3464 is identical across themes; the gradient debit-card hero glows against the dark navy. Transaction emoji avatars and Pot coins remain full-color and saturated — only chrome (dividers, secondary text) recedes. Income amounts stay green #2FCB8F; the feed is the soul of the app and never dims.
