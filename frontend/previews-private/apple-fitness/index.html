Dark Mode Notes

Apple Fitness is dark-first on iPhone: the canvas is true black #000000 so the three glowing rings are the brightest thing on screen. Grouped cards lift to system #1C1C1E. The ring colors are the entire brand — Move #FA114F, Exercise #92E82A, Stand #1EE4E1 — and they never change across themes; each ring sits on a 22%-opacity track of its own color. Labels brighten slightly on black (Move label #FF375F). Text uses Apple's label-on-dark opacity ramp: primary 100%, secondary 60%, tertiary 30%. Fitness+ artwork is full-color and cinematic; the rings glow.
