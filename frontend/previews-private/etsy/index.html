Warm Light Notes

Etsy's canvas is a paper-warm #FAF5EF cream — the handmade brand signature, never stark white. White product cards float on the cream with a soft warm-tinted (brown) shadow so they read like prints on paper, not SKUs in cold space. Etsy Orange #F1641E is the only accent: action, active tab, sale, and the favorite heart. The heart is the emotional core — it bounces (1.0→1.25→1.0) and fills orange on tap. Review stars are near-black #222222, never gold. CTAs are full-pill and friendly.
