Dark Mode Notes

Garmin Connect is dark-first and uses true black #000000 as the canvas — it makes the GPS route line and HR-zone colors pop and saves OLED power on watch-paired phones. Cards lift to #121417. Garmin Blue #007CC3 is the only brand color; on black it is brightened to #2A9FD6 for text/active states to clear WCAG AA. Numerals are Roboto Condensed so long metric values (pace, distance, elevation) stay on one line. Data-viz colors (Body Battery blue, the green→gold→orange→red HR ramp) never change across themes — only chrome dims.
