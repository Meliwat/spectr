Dark Mode Notes

Crunchyroll is OLED-first — there is no light mode. Canvas is true black #000000 so key-art bleeds edge-to-edge and the hero scrim resolves to a perfect black floor. Surfaces lift to #16171A / #23252B for sheets and rows. Crunchyroll Orange #F47521 is the only accent — used for the primary CTA, the active tab, the segmented underline, and the episode-progress bar. Premium Gold #FFC107 appears only on the Premium badge. Posters and thumbnails stay full-color; chrome recedes so the art is the interface.
