Dark Mode Notes

Schwab's dark mode is a deep maritime navy, not pure black — canvas sits at #0A1622 with cards lifting to #11212F and #18303F. The account hero keeps its #003B5C navy gradient even on dark, preserving the brand's banker-blue identity. Schwab Blue #009DDC is the primary action; the brighter #46BEEC Sky handles links and selected tabs where contrast on the dark field matters. Gain/loss color is reserved strictly for financial semantics — gain #18B07B, loss #E2564E — and never used decoratively. All money values use tabular figures so balances, prices, and quantities align down every column. The trade ticket is the visual centerpiece: a segmented Buy/Sell control over right-aligned tabular fields and an estimated-cost summary.
