Dark Mode Notes

Crypto.com is dark-first with a distinctly navy bias — the canvas #03060F and surfaces #0B1426 carry a cool blue undertone, not neutral gray. Brand navy #002D74/#103F68 appears in hero gradients and banners; electric blue #1199FA is the interactive accent for CTAs, links, and the active tab. The metallic Visa card renders identically in both themes — its gradient is the brand centerpiece. Market semantics green #00C08B / red #F6485D never invert. The light theme swaps canvas to #FFFFFF and text to #0A1F44 while keeping accent, navy, and card metals unchanged. All monetary values use Roboto Mono with tabular figures.
