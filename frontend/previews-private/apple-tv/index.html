Dark Mode Notes

The Apple TV app is true-black #000000 — not a charcoal — so OLED pixels switch off entirely and inset rounded artwork appears to float. It is the most restrained of all streamers: SF Pro typography, generous whitespace, and glass-and-blur chrome (the tab bar is a translucent blur(20px) material over true black). System blue #0A84FF is the only accent — it tints the active tab and text links — while the primary "Play" button is solid white #FFFFFF with a black label, and secondary actions use the iOS translucent fill rgba(120,120,128,0.36). The signature Up Next rail uses 16:9 thumbnails with a thin white resume-progress bar; MLS Season Pass content carries its hot-pink #ED1A6F, and live sports get red #FF453A. Surfaces step up from #000 → #1C1C1E → #2C2C2E.
