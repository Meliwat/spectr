Dark Mode Notes

Swiggy's dark mode keeps a near-black canvas (#121212) with cards lifting to #1C1C1E. Swiggy Orange #FC8019 is unchanged across themes — the warm orange stays appetizing on dark and drives every primary CTA, footer accent, and active tab. The rating green #48C479 and dashed dividers stay constant. Food photography remains full-color, full-saturation — the dark UI chrome recedes so the dish imagery and the orange CTA carry the screen.
