Dark Mode Notes

Peloton is born-dark: the canvas is pure black #000000 so cinematic class thumbnails and the studio video feed bleed edge-to-edge. Cards lift to #121212. Peloton Red #DF1E2E is the only brand color — used for the LIVE badge, the primary "Take Class" button, the output ring, and your highlighted leaderboard row; on black it brightens to #FF4B57 for text/active to clear AA. In-class metric colors (cadence red-orange, resistance amber, Strive/HR blue) are fixed semantics and never change theme. There is no light mode — the app is always studio-black.
