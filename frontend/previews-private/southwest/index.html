Dark Mode Notes

Southwest's dark theme keeps the heart tri-color exactly. Canvas drops to deep navy #0E1726 (a darkened Southwest Blue), cards lift to #16223A. Warm Yellow #F9B612 stays identical — it is the primary CTA and the boarding-position color on both themes. Bold Red #E51D23 is reserved strictly for alerts and cancellations, never decoration. Blue shifts brighter to #2567E8 for links so it clears WCAG AA on the navy canvas. The boarding-position readout is always the loudest element on screen.
