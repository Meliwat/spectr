Dark Mode Notes

Pluto TV is dark-only — a deep cool navy #0B0F1F, not pure black, that evokes a late-night TV glow. The EPG grid is the product: the fixed channel column sits on #141A2E, idle program cells on #141A2E, and the "On Now" cell lifts to #1C2440 with a 3pt #FFE100 yellow left border — the single most important state in the app. Pluto Blue #0048FF drives primary CTAs and the active category pill; the yellow is reserved for "live/now" emphasis and the active tab. The live indicator is always a #FF3B5C dot + "LIVE" label. Video plays full-color in the mini-player; chrome stays dim.
