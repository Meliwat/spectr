Dark Mode Notes

Zalo's dark mode keeps the brand intact: headers and the active tab stay Zalo Blue #0068FF — the blue reads well on dark. The chat backdrop flips from soft blue-grey #E8ECF1 to #101418; incoming bubbles become #1F2329 with light text, outgoing bubbles shift to a deep Zalo-blue tint #0A3A7A with white text (the asymmetric clipped-tail corner is preserved). Content surfaces drop to #15171A; dividers to #262A30. The notification red #F5325B and mini-app icon colors stay identical. Vietnamese diacritics must always have full ascender/descender room — never clip the line box.
