Fluid Dark

Feeld is dark-native: a pure black canvas (#000000) with one electric accent — acid-yellow #E8FF63 — and a warm pink (#FF5C8A) for desire. The identity is fluid and non-binary; type is Space Grotesk's quirky humanist geometry. Cards are deeply rounded (28px), buttons are full pills, and the acid only ever lands on the one primary action.

Dark Mode Notes

Feeld is dark-first by identity, not as an option — the canvas is true black #000000 so profile photography and the acid accent feel like neon on velvet. Acid-yellow #E8FF63 is rationed: it appears on exactly one primary action per screen (the Like button, the active tab dot) and never as a fill behind text-heavy areas. Desire pink #FF5C8A is the secondary emotional color. Surfaces lift in tiny steps (#121212 → #1C1C1E) and dividers stay at #2A2A2A hairlines. There is no light mode in the shipping app.
