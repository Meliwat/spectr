Dark Mode Notes

Sleep Cycle is dark-first by nature — it's a bedside app. The canvas is never pure black; it's a deep indigo #14152E beneath the night gradient (#2A2D5A → #3B4371) so the screen glows softly rather than glaring at 3am. Cards lift to #1E2046. Aqua #4FD1E6 is the only high-luminance accent and is used sparingly on the active wave and primary stats. Light mode swaps to a pale dawn (#F4F5FC) with the same indigo accent — the sleep graph keeps its stage colors in both modes. Brightness auto-dims further during a tracking session.
