Dark Mode Notes

Panera's identity is bakery-warm, so dark mode is a warm near-black #14140F — never a cold gray. Cards lift to #1E1E18. Panera Green #4C8B2B is slightly too dark to read on the dark canvas, so the bright variant #6BBE45 carries CTAs, active tabs, the MyPanera progress fill, and reward tags. Warm cream #F4EFE1 inverts to #F3EFE4 text. Food photography stays full-color and is the brightest thing on screen — appetite is the priority.
