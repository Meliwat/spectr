Dark Mode Notes

KakaoTalk's dark mode keeps the brand yellow on the things that matter: outbound bubbles stay Kakao Yellow #FEE500 with deep brown #3C1E1E text — the contrast reads cleanly on both themes. The chat thread backdrop shifts from light gray to a desaturated blue-gray #1E2A33 (the dark equivalent of KakaoTalk's signature thread color); inbound bubbles sit at #2E2E2E. Friends/list canvas is #1A1A1A (warm, not pure black). The yellow unread mark and time dock to the bubble's inner side, not inside it.
