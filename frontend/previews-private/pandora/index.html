Dark Mode Notes

Pandora's app is dark-first. The canvas is a deep blue-navy #0B0F1C derived from Pandora Blue #224099, never a neutral gray. Now Playing is a top-down gradient pulled from the album art into the navy floor — chrome floats over it with no card frame. Bright Blue #3668FF is the constant: the active thumb-up, the play button, the scrubber fill, and every primary action. The thumb-up fills bright blue when active; thumb-down stays a hollow secondary outline — the asymmetry signals that a thumb-up shapes the station while a thumb-down skips and tunes away. Album artwork is never tinted; only the surrounding UI shifts.
