Dark Mode Notes

Chime's dark mode keeps the canvas a deep green-charcoal (#0E1411), not neutral black — the brand's mint warmth carries through. The balance hero stays a full #1EC677 → #12A862 gradient card with near-black #062014 text for maximum contrast. SpotMe and incoming money use a brighter mint #2EE6A6 so positive amounts glow on dark; declines and negative balances use coral #FF6B6B, pending uses amber #FFB23E. Mint-soft fills shift from a pale tint to #123A2A. Card surfaces lift to #16201B.
