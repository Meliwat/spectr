Dark Mode Notes

Coffee Meets Bagel's dark mode is a cozy coffee-house, not a black void. The canvas is a deep roasted brown-black #14100D with cards lifted to #1F1813 — warm, never neutral grey. Bagel-orange #F4623A stays the primary action color (the big Like button, active tab, badges) and reads warmly on the dark roast. Roasted brew-brown #A0522D is the secondary brand color for "Send a Bagel" and selected chips. Cream #F3E4CF replaces white for on-photo text and outlines so nothing feels stark. Profile photography stays full-color and full-bleed inside the 24pt-radius bagel card — the person is the hero; the warm chrome only frames them.
