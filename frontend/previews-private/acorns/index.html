Dark Mode Notes

Acorns' dark mode is a deep aubergine, not pure black — canvas sits at #14121A, tinted from Acorns Oak #5A2B82. Surfaces lift to #1E1B27. Acorn Green #6FBF4E is the action color and the growth/positive signal — it stays vivid across themes. The portfolio donut keeps its full four-color allocation palette; the Round-Ups card stays purple; Found Money amounts stay green. Only chrome (dividers, secondary text) recedes — the data visualization never does.
