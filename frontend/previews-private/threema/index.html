Dark Mode Notes

Threema's dark mode keeps the privacy-first calm: canvas at #121212, surfaces lifting to #1C1C1E. Threema Green #088A29 brightens to #1FA53C for nav tint and links so it stays legible on dark. Outgoing bubbles deepen to #0C5E22 while incoming sit on neutral #262628. The trust-level dots (#E5453A / #EF8B2C / #15A33A) keep their hue across themes — verification status must read identically in light and dark.
