Dark Mode Notes

Flickr is photography-first, so the dark canvas sits at near-black #0C0D0E — darker than most apps — letting full-color photos dominate the justified mosaic with only 3px gutters of negative space. Cards lift to #1A1B1D. Flickr Pink #FF0084 and Flickr Blue #0063DC are identical across themes — the saturated dual brand reads beautifully on both. Photos never dim; only chrome, EXIF tables, and dividers (#2A2C30) recede. Light mode flips canvas to #FFFFFF with text at #1C1F23.
