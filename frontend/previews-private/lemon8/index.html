Dark Mode Notes

Lemon8 is unapologetically light-first — it's a magazine, and the bright white canvas (#FFFFFF) is core to its editorial, Pinterest-meets-RED aesthetic. In dark mode the canvas drops to #121212 with cards at #1C1C1E, but the pastel topic tags shift to muted dark equivalents (mint #1F3A2C, blush #3A2630, sky #1F2F42) so they stay legible without glowing. Lemon8 Yellow #FFE600 is identical across themes and stays paired only with ink #1A1A1A text — never white-on-yellow. Photography in cards never dims.
