Dark Mode Notes

Craft's dark mode is a soft, near-charcoal #1A1A1E — never pure black — with block cards lifting to #232328. The blue→purple gradient (#2F5BEA → #6E56CF) is identical across themes; it's the one constant brand signature on the New Document button, FAB, and active tab. Text inverts to a soft white #ECECEF, preserving the editorial, paper-like reading rhythm. Block cards keep their generous 12pt radius and soft shadows; covers and emoji stay full-color while chrome recedes.
