Dark Mode Notes

Evernote's dark mode is a true near-black reading surface — canvas #1C1C1E, notes lift to #242426, toolbars to #2E2E30. Evernote Green #00A82D stays for the brand FAB and logo, but interactive green brightens to #2DBE60 so checkbox fills, links, and the active tab clear the dark canvas at WCAG AA. Tags become green-tinted pills (#1C3A24 bg / #5FD68A text). Body text never goes pure white — primary is #E4E4E6 for long-form reading comfort; the warm #1C2B33 ink of light mode is inverted.
