Light Mode Notes

Hopper's canvas is pure #FFFFFF — a bright, data-forward decision surface. The calendar heatmap is the core mechanic: cells run a strict scale from buy-green #34C759 through neutral #F5F5F7 to wait-red #FA4747. Cells stay flat — their color is the signal, never shadow. Hopper Red #FA4747 drives the CTA, active tab, and bunny; buy-green is reserved for positive verdicts and price drops. Every predicted price is paired with a colored "BUY"/"WAIT" pill so the decision is the strongest glance on the screen.
