Dark Mode Notes

Zomato's dark mode keeps a warm near-black canvas at #121212; cards lift to #1C1C1E. Zomato Red #E23744 stays the primary action color across themes. The rating system keeps its semantic scale — green #267E3E (great), amber #DB7C38 (average), red #E23744 (poor) — so a pill's color always means the same thing. Veg/non-veg marks keep their green/maroon squares. Food photography is never dimmed; only chrome and dividers darken.
