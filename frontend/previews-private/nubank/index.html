Dark Mode Notes

Nubank's dark mode is a deep aubergine, not pure black — canvas sits at #15101C, tinted from the brand Nu Purple #820AD1. The full-bleed purple hero header is identical across themes; it's the brand's signature first impression. Account and credit-card tiles lift to #1F1729. Purple actions stay vivid; on dark, primary text and icons shift to a soft purple-tinted off-white. Positive amounts use green #2ED47A. Chrome recedes; the purple never does.
