Dark Mode Notes

SoFi is a dark-native brand — the deep navy #0A0E27 is the canonical canvas in both themes (light mode lifts surfaces to white but the marketing identity stays navy + electric blue). The member hero is a signature #00A0DF → #1B53C4 → #0A0E27 gradient with white text. SoFi Blue #00A0DF brightens to #29C2FF for accents, links, and active tabs so it pops on navy. Investment gains are green #2FD08A, losses coral #FF6B6B, rewards gold #F2C14E. Surfaces lift navy → #121736 → #1B2147; soft blue fills sit at #0E2A44.
