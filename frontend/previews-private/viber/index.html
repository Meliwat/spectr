Dark Mode Notes

Viber's dark mode uses a deep aubergine-tinted black (#121118) rather than neutral gray — a subtle nod to the purple brand. The signature Viber Purple #7360F2 outgoing bubble is identical across themes; incoming bubbles invert from light #EDEBF5 to #26232F. The free-call banner keeps its purple→deep-violet (#59267C) gradient. Seen receipts use violet #8F7DF7; the online indicator stays green #46C26A. Surfaces dim; brand purple and content do not.
