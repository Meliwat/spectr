Distinctive — Fluent Acrylic + Streaming Shimmer
Acrylic surfaces are frosted, tinted, and blurred so they float over the conversation. As an answer streams, a warm flourish-gradient highlight sweeps the leading edge…

The defining material is Fluent acrylic — the prompt bar, sidebar, chips, and answer cards are blurred + tinted layers (you can see content faintly through them). Surfaces blur-in over 220ms; answers stream token-by-token with a warm #FF6F61 → #FFB900 shimmer. The send button morphs to a blue stop square mid-stream.

Light + Dark Notes

Copilot is a warm, layered Fluent workspace — not a bare document. Answers live in soft acrylic cards (16pt radius, whisper shadow) on a clean canvas. Dark mode inverts canvas to #202020 and surface to #2D2D2D; links/send use #4DA3E0 for AA contrast. Fluent primaries use an 8pt rounded rect (the tone selector is the pill). The flourish stops (#FF6F61 → #FFB900) stay identical across themes — brand only, never a body-text tint or button fill.
