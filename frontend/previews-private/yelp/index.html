Dark Mode Notes

Yelp's dark mode keeps the canvas near-black (#161616) with cards lifting to #1E1E1E. The brand Red #FF1A1A stays saturated for primary actions and the active tab. The 5-star scale uses Orange #F25C05 in both modes — it stays legible on dark where pure red stars would muddy. Section separators become 8px #0C0C0C bands instead of the light-mode gray fills. Links lighten from #0073BB to #4FA3D9; business photos and user avatars stay full-color.
