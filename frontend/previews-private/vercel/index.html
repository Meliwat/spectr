Dark Mode Notes

Vercel is dark-mode-native and ships true black #000000 — not the charcoal most apps use. Cards lift only one step to #0A0A0A; separation comes from hairline #2E2E2E borders, not shadows. The system is deliberately monochrome — the only color is the status traffic-light (#0CCE6B Ready / #F5A623 Building / #E5484D Error) and #0070F3 for links and primary blue actions. Light mode inverts to pure white #FFFFFF with #000000 text; the geometric triangle logomark flips fill but never changes shape.
