Light Mode Notes

Locket is warm and light by design — there is no dark mode in spirit. The world is a soft cream→peach gradient (#FFE9CC → #FFD9A8) over cream canvas (#FFF7EC); Locket Gold (#FFB02E) is the single brand accent, with a deeper amber (#F08A1D) for text-on-cream and warm coral (#FF7A59) as a friendly secondary. The product is widget-first: the real surface is the Home Screen widget showing a friend's square photo. In-app, the signature trio is the square full-bleed friend photo (very round 40px corners), the big white circular capture button with a gold inner ring, and the 4-up friends photo-history grid. Type is Poppins — warm, rounded, geometric. Shadows are warm honey-tinted (rgba(180,120,30,…)), never gray. Everything is intimate, close-friends-only, and cozy.
