Dark Mode Notes

Pocket Casts is dark-first. The canvas is a true near-black #1A1A1A with cards lifting to #232323. The brand Red #F43E37 anchors tab bar selection and Subscribe CTAs, but the Now Playing screen swaps to a per-podcast theme tint sampled from the cover art (here #E0533C) — it re-colors the scrubber fill, the circular play button, and active action chips. Cover art renders full-color and large; chrome stays monochrome so artwork dominates. A light theme exists, but the dark theme is the canonical Pocket Casts identity.
